VISION

In pursuit of perfection, the University of Baguio is committed to provide balanced quality education by nurturing academic execellence, relevant social skills and ethical values in a fun learning environment.

MISSION

The University of Baguio educates indiviuals to be empowered proffessionals in the global community. The UB High School prepares the students to be competent in attaining higher education.

OBJECTIVES

The UB High School, through a wholesome and dynamic leaning atmosphere, aims to produce a graduate who:
1. exhibits a distinctive level of academic proficiency in pursuit of higher learning;
2. applies acquired significant skills in different areas of learning;
3. upholds moral and spiritual values;
4. conducts investigatory researches;
5. engages in sustaintable outreach programs; and
6. practices sound decision-making in the choice of career.
