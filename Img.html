They give students two types of camping. One is a Survival Camping and the other one is Jamboree The Survival Camping is open for all levels and the Jamboree is for the scouts of the campus.

It is for the students who belongs under the scouting SPAS. It is also an extra curricular for the students. They can also join in the paredes like opening for Panpanagbenga festival or during the opening
